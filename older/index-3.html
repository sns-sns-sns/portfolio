Auf Etwas is a Berlin-based creative studio focusing on digital art and culture, where we push the boundaries of sonic conventions, foster inclusivity, drive social impact and provide a platform for diverse voices.

Established in 2011 in Athens, our journey began in the music industry, evolving into artist management, artist liaison, and stage management for high-end artists and venues. Over time, our expertise grew, leading us to venture into full-length films, film festivals, and fashion productions while collaborating with Berlinale, Cannes Film Festival, and Berlin Fashion Week.

Driven by a vision to revolutionize the creative industry, we aim to bridge diverse disciplines and create a platform for untold stories while ensuring the appropriate representation in front and behind the camera. Through launching our documentary-style video campaigns for MTV, we have started capturing authentic narratives that address current social taboos and forge profound connections with communities.

Led by Eva Mikelatou, an Athens-born, Berlin-based creative producer with a background in music, film, and psychology. Whether it's through sound, film, or other multimedia, Auf Etwas is dedicated to empowering underrepresented communities and redefining the way people experience culture by combining innovative and experimental approaches.
